PART I: THE MISSION MATH (1,500 CALORIE DEFICIT)

At 260 lbs, your body burns ~2,400 calories just existing. We are adding 1,500 active burn calories on top. This is how we cook the fat off.

THE 1,500 CALORIE BREAKDOWN

You cannot get this all at once. You must attack it in three waves.

WAVE 1: THE MORNING PATROL (Completed)

Activity: 3-Mile Fasted Walk.
Yield: ~400 Calories.
Status: Mandatory. Non-negotiable.

WAVE 2: THE HOURLY TAX (The "Gap" Filler)

Activity: Hourly Micro-Workouts (The "Pain Currency").
Frequency: Every hour on the hour (10x per day).
Yield: ~600 Calories.

Note: At your weight, simply standing up and doing 20 squats + 20 pushups burns significant fuel.

WAVE 3: THE EVENING FINISHER

Activity: Rucking (Pack Walk) OR Stationary Bike.
Duration: 45–60 Minutes.
Yield: ~500 Calories.

Note: This closes the gap. If you skip this, you miss the target.

PART III: THE PAIN WALL (PSYCHOLOGY)

Motivation fades. Discipline needs a visual anchor.

SETUP:
  • The Board: Corkboard or section of wall in your garage/office.
  • The Content: Photos of you at your heaviest, medical bills, old injuries.
  • The Why: Photos of your wife and child.
  • The Goal: A picture of the physique/life you want.
  • The Chart: This document, pinned in the center.
THE RITUAL:

Morning: Stand in front of the wall for 60 seconds. Look at the "Pain" photos. Remind yourself what happens if you stay the same.

The Urge: When you want to quit or binge eat, touch the wall. Physical contact breaks the mental loop.

PART V: RULES OF ENGAGEMENT
  • NO NEGOTIATION: The chart is the law. If you eat it, you pay for it.
  • EMBRACE THE SUCK: You are overweight and in pain. The workouts will hurt. Being fat hurts. Choose your pain.
  • MODIFY, DON'T QUIT: If your knees scream, do pushups. If your shoulders scream, do squats. Movement is mandatory.
  • RESET: If you fail a day, you do not spiral. You wake up at 0500 and restart.
